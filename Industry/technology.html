    • Technology Industry

    Global technology companies are increasingly setting bold sustainability transformation goals. However, according to the CDP, less than 25% of companies are on track to achieve sustainability targets. Success rests on managing and mitigating risk by aligning stakeholders’ ambitions with a bold and actionable pathway forward. To stay on track in a dynamic industry, technology companies must build strategic roadmaps, leveraging digital tools, financing expertise, operational leadership, and engineering capabilities to achieve ambitious decarbonization goals.

    Sustainability in the Technology Industry?

    Carboless Impact helps to decarbonize companies across the entire technology value chain, including software and platforms, network and equipment, semiconductors, technology manufacturing, and media and telecommunication. We are purpose-built to enable transformation at speed and scale for technology companies worldwide, at any stage of their sustainability journey.

    How Carboless Impact Can Help Tech Companies

    Getting up to speed on driving sustainability in the technology industry.

    • GHG Footprint Measurement and Reporting: Collect end-to-end measurements and data of organization emissions to drive decision-making and set targets.
    • Energy Efficiency Solutions: Conduct energy efficiency workshops to build competencies across facilities, enabling teams to identify projects with shorter paybacks and clear cost savings; these programs can be entirely self-funding, requiring no upfront capital from suppliers.

    Carboless holdings Pte Ltd is a large supporter in the fight against global warming. Our technology helps businesses to tackle their own carbon emissions without incurring a huge cost. The Carbon Management Platform allows companies to manage their CO2 emissions and to take steps to reduce them. Together, our work can elicit the global impact necessary to fight against climate change.
